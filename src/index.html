The African elephant is the largest land animal on Earth and is known for its intelligence, social behavior, and long tusks.

There are five species of rhinoceros, all of which are large herbivores with thick skin and one or two horns on their snout.

The leopard is a solitary big cat known for its beautiful spotted coat and its stealthy hunting skills.

The lion is the king of the jungle and is known for its social organization, its powerful roar, and its mane.

The African buffalo is a large herbivore that lives in herds and is known for its aggressive temperament.
